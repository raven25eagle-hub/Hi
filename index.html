The letter I wrote
May 9, 2026 · Graduation Day
To the one who made ordinary days lighter,

I've liked you since first year, though "nagka-crush sa'yo" is probably the more honest way to put it. For a long time, I chose to keep that quietly to myself. Most of what I felt stayed quiet, but it stayed real. Even from a distance, your presence gave me a kind of inspiration that made ordinary days feel lighter... not until this happened HAHAHA.

There was a time when napunta yung usapan namin sa mga crush sa school, and to go along with the conversation, I ended up mentioning mine and yes, it was you. Somehow the story spread, madaldal kasi tong isa kong kaibigan yung naka hikari suncreen na nag mukhang bading pag gamit nya HAHAHA so hanggang sa ayun, umabot kina Jash at Sir Kim yung kwento.

Kaya ayun, napunta rin sa point na nabanggit ni Jash na kilala ka raw niya, at sinabihan niya akong i-add ka sa Facebook. Kaya ayun, inadd kita, though I didn't expect na i-aaccept mo ako HAHAHA.

Nung first year kasi, malayong paghanga lang talaga. Sapat na sa akin na nakikita lang kita sa school noon. Pero nung nag-second year, doon ko naramdaman na hindi lang siya basta dumaang crush naging consistent lang talaga yung pagka-crush ko sa'yo habang tumatagal.

Habang mas napapansin kita, doon ko rin nakita kung gaano ka ka-genuine, ka-sincere, at ka-kind as a person. Though honestly, medyo natawa rin ako nung sinabi mong mas makulit ka raw kapag mas nakikilala ka kasi sobrang layo nun sa first impression ko sayo HAHAHA. Pero I think that made me admire you even more kasi behind that calm and quiet image, may softer and more playful side ka rin pala. And slowly, that simple crush turned into something deeper.

Actually, almost two years din akong hanggang tahimik lang HAHAHA. Then one month before graduation, doon lang talaga ako nagkaroon ng courage na lumapit at makapag-picture kasama ka. and brief conversation lang naman talaga siya, pero honestly, malaking bagay na yun para sa akin.

Imagine, almost two years kitang naging crush tapos bigla akong nagkaroon ng courage na lumapit at makapag-picture kasama ka. Parang hindi pa graduation day, graduate na agad ako HAHAHAHA.

Saka honestly, first time ko rin naman magka crush sa isang tao nang ganito katagal, kaya siguro medyo naging awkward din ako minsan HAHAHA. I was never really the type of person na marunong maging expressive pagdating sa ganitong bagay, so most of the time, tahimik lang talaga ako.

Kaya siguro inabot din ako ng graduation bago ko nagawang sabihin ito nang maayos. Hindi dahil may ine-expect ako in return, but because I wanted to say it with complete honesty and respect.

Okay teka muna HAHAHA. Feeling ko habang tumatagal itong letter, tumitigas na yang kamao mo at baka mamaya masapak mo na ako through the screen sa sobrang dami kong sinasabi.

Ok so kidding aside, I also want to thank you for the small kindnesses you probably never thought twice about. Yung simpleng pag-accept mo sa Facebook, yung mga usap natin sa Messenger, kahit maiikling conversations lang, those became moments I appreciated more than I ever showed.

Siguro ang simple pakinggan nung lagi mong sinasabi na “bahay at kapilya lang” ginagawa mo, pero honestly, that was one of the things that made me admire you even more. There was something so sincere and comforting about the way you devoted your time to your church duties and carried yourself with quiet kindness.

In a world where a lot of people try so hard to impress others, you stood out to me because you felt genuine. And maybe that’s when I realized it stopped being just a crush. I slowly started falling for the person you are, not just the person I see.

And the more I quietly admired you, the more I found myself wanting to know you beyond the small conversations and random moments we had. Hindi dahil naghahanap ako ng perfect na tao, pero because there was something about you that felt peaceful, sincere, and comforting in a way that made me genuinely want to understand you more.

I am saying this now not to disturb your peace. gusto ko lang talaga maging honest before this chapter completely ends, because I didn’t want to graduate carrying feelings I never even had the courage to admit

Some people pass through our lives quietly, pero somehow nagiging malaking parte sila ng memories natin without them even realizing it. And for me, you became one of those people. Through the small moments,short conversations, and simple interactions, you slowly became someone I genuinely admired more than I ever showed.

Siguro, this letter is just my way of freeing myself from the weight ng mga bagay na matagal kong hindi nasabi. No expectations at all gusto ko lang talagang maging totoo sa'yo at sa sarili ko bago matapos ang chapter na 'to. because some feelings deserve honesty before they become just another memory.

— Paolo
